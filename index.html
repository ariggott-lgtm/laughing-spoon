2026 OKRs

The Design team's 2026 objectives and key measurements.

L&D Content Organization: Design

Partner with the DPL team on the L&D content organization project, specifically naming conventions, content navigation UX, permissions, and backend storage, to reduce content friction, improve usability, and enable consistent training delivery at scale.

Key Measurements
  • UX proposal delivered for L&D content organization and navigation structure
  • Naming convention framework designed and ready for DPL review and adoption
  • Permission and backend storage design recommendations submitted
  • Wireframes and UX flows in development to support hiring-ready training infrastructure
  • Design feedback loop established with DPL and Operations stakeholders
Conservice Core: Design

Partner with the Training and LEAD teams to organize, update, and build a unified onboarding ecosystem spanning LMS pathways, KB, resources, and manager tools, delivering a consistent experience across all role levels.

Key Measurements
  • Update Day 1 & Day 2 content
  • Full curriculum overhaul of foundations training
  • Curriculum improvements of professional team onboarding
  • Build external leadership onboarding experience
Leadership Elevated (STL): Design

Partner with the LEAD team to design a comprehensive 12-month leadership development program for the STL level: building the curriculum, LMS pathway, VILT experiences, peer projects, and resources to meaningfully elevate frontline leadership skills.

Key Measurements
  • LMS pathway
  • Project set-up & updates in Monday
  • 4 VILT
  • 3 Peer Projects
  • 1 Development Plan
  • Pre & post work
  • Resource access
  • Communications
